Who we are

Built on
experimentation

EXPLAB is a community in Tunis for artists, engineers, musicians, and designers who believe the most interesting work happens at the intersection of disciplines.

We gather around creative coding, sensors, live A/V, generative visuals, hardware, and whatever tools let us connect ideas to physical reality. We experiment in public.

We are not a school. Not a startup. Not a brand. We are a group of people who show up.

Meetups & Workshops
Regular gatherings in Tunis — structured workshops, open labs, and critique sessions around new media tools.
Skill Mapping
We track what the community knows and what it needs — connecting people with complementary skills across disciplines.
Open Collaboration
Projects emerge from within the community. No hierarchy — just people working on things that interest them together.
Community Manifesto
We write our own values together — at gatherings, in public, as a live document that evolves as the community does.
