Who We Are
About EXPLAB · Mission · How to participate

EXPLAB is a community for new media arts and interactive experimentation in Tunis, Tunisia — centered around creative coding and the tools that bridge digital ideas and the physical world.

Mission. We meet, build, and experiment together: meetups, workshops, and open labs where individual practice becomes collective knowledge and shared tools become a shared language.

How to participate. Come to a gathering or workshop, map your skills and the hardware you have, write the manifesto with us, and start or join an experiment. Everyone contributes something — curiosity counts.
