Code of Conduct
About Mouhit Space

We kindly ask all participants attending the gatherings at Mouhit to respect the space's Code of Conduct and rules to ensure a safe and welcoming environment for everyone. This is a shared space where everybody is expected to treat others with due respect and mindfulness.

Please read the Code of Conduct thoroughly and adhere to its guidelines when signing up for membership.

⬇
Mouhit Code of Conduct
PDF · Download and read before agreeing
→
Key rules at a glance
Mouhit does not discriminate on the basis of nationality, gender, religion, age, disability or sexual orientation.
Zero tolerance for harassment, intimidation, discrimination, aggression, or bullying — in person or online.
Strictly no illegal drugs at any time. No alcohol during public events.
Smoking is strictly prohibited indoors. Use outdoor ashtrays.
Leave the space as you found it. Wash up after yourself. Help clean after events.
Respect Mouhit's equipment, furniture, and community areas.
If you witness harm, report immediately to a Mouhit representative — do not handle it alone.
Violations may result in immediate removal from the event or space.
Please read and agree to the Code of Conduct to continue.
